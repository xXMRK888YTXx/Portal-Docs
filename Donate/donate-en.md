Financial support is not the only way to contribute! You can also help by:

1.  **Providing Feedback:** Let us know what works well and what could be improved. Feel free to open **[Issues (Android)](https://github.com/xXMRK888YTXx/Portal-Android)** or **[Issues (PC)](https://github.com/KoksMen/Portal-Windows)** in our repositories.
